Disconnected in depression

Depression is commonly characterized by persistent sadness, negative thoughts about oneself or life. But another common aspect of depression is the feeling of loneliness or disconnection from other people. Although depression can exacerbate feelings of loneliness, one of the fastest and best ways to feel better, ironically, is to talk about how you are feeling. Which is difficult when feeling disconnected.

Disconnected gray

Gray can be perceived as emotionally distant or disconnected. Since gray is neither one thing nor the other(not black and not white) we can think of it as indecisive, which is similair to the anxiety you have when being depressed.
