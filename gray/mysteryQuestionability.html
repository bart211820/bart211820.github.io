Questionability

What is questionability

If you say that something is questionable, you mean that it is not completely honest, reasonable, or acceptable.

Gray's relation to questionability

There is a saying that goes: "Not everything is black or white", meaning not everything is one thing or the other. Not everything is good or bad, or right or wrong, there can be shades of gray inbetween. Gray is associated with morally questionable matters, these are actions that aren't illigal but still deemed wrong by the majority of society.

Shades of gray

All shades of gray can be related to questionability, however the more in the middle of the spectrum the color is the more questionable it can get, since it's not leaning to a specific side.
