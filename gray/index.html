What is this website?

This website is all about the color gray. What does the color gray represent and what kind of emotions does it invoke?

People tend to overlook gray as a color because they think it's not that interesting, but when you actually look deeper you will find that it actually has quite a few interesting characteristics. If you want to know what these characteristics are, you're gonna have to click on one of the links above or you can find a subject by using the filter option on the top right corner of the page.
