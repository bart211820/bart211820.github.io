Indecisiveness

Being indecisive can be exhausting – you’re constantly caught up in a cycle of overthinking, always chasing thoughts, and feel plagued by worry no matter how small the issue to be resolved. It sucks your energy, leaving you feeling drained, disconnected, and off balance.

Gray's relation to Indecisiveness

The thing with gray is, that you could also see it as indecisive. It's inbetween black and white and doesn't even know itself which color it wants to be. This way you can connect the color gray to your own struggle with making decisions. Do you pick option 1 or 2? Do you pick black or white? You are stuck in the gray area.
